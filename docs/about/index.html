About Us.

A Press: the art or business of printing and publishing. Taken a step further a press is about sharing information.
A Context Driven Press is about doing the best we can to share information. The biggest constraint being our own understanding.
Context Driven Press serves as a platform for sharing information and ideas across numerous properties across the web.

In the Past


In the not too distant past, Context Driven Press was run by Cem Kaner and Rebecca Fiedler.
Together they published a number of software testing books that are no longer in print.
